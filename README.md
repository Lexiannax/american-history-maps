American History Maps
Interactive Historical Atlas 1607–1877
An interactive map of the United States tracking the legal and political status of every state from the British colonial period through the end of Reconstruction.
Seven Eras Covered

1607 — Colonial Charters
1765 — Sons of Liberty
1781 — Articles of Confederation
1787 — Constitution and Expansion
1854 — Bleeding Kansas
1861 — Civil War
1865 — Reconstruction and the 13th Amendment

Built With
D3.js, TopoJSON, vanilla HTML and JavaScript.

History 17A, City College of San Francisco, Spring 2026.
